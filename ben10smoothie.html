Ben 10 Smoothie Reciepe

Make the fan favoruite ben 10 smoothie

  • 2 Fresh Nectarines
  • 1/2 Cup Soymilk or Almond Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Vanilla Yogurt or Greek Yogurt
  • 10 Ice Cubes
  • Honey (To taste) k
Directions
  1. Cut the nectarines into chunks & place the chunks into a plastic sandwich baggie & freeze overnight.
  2. Dump the frozen nectarine bits into a blender & add the yogurt & milk.
  3. Blend until the nectarines chunks are no lunger chunky & all of the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  4. iGive it a taste & blend in some honey for sweetness if you'd like.
  5. You can also add in the optional ice cubes if you want tiny cold crunchiness.
