Garfield Lasagna Reciepe


Garfield's legendary reciepe is a stuff of legend. Learn how to make the reciepe:
Item List
  • large cans of peeled tomatoes.
  • 2 onions diced
  • 1 spoon of sugar
  • 1 sprig of thyme, finely chopped
  • 100g butter
  • 23 tablespoons flour
  • Milk
  • Salt, pepper, tabasco and nutmeg
  • 1k minced meat
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 200g of grated Gouda cheese
  • Oil, salt, pepper and paprika
Here is the direction for the reciepe
  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Make tomato sause
  3. Saute the minced meat
  4. Make the Bechamel
  5. Boil Pasta
  6. Assemble the Lasagna
